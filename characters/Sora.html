Sora

UA：Sword
You have 3 UAs. During Setup, begin the game with this one. You may use this action during your Option Phase: (2) Clear a bullet in one of the bottom 2 rows of your sight.
UA2：Bazooka
You may use this action during your Option Phase: (2) Clear a bullet in one of the middle 2 rows of your sight.
UA3：Beam Rifle
You may use this action during your Option Phase: (2) Clear a bullet in one of the top 2 rows of your sight.
Abilities
(1) Move a bullet left, right, or down 1 space. (2) Draw a pattern. (2) Gain your Beam Rifle or Bazooka UA. (★) You may swap 2 bullets in your sight. (★) Gain 1 action point.
